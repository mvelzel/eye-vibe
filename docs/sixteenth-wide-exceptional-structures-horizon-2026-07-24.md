## Fresh exact observations

Every nonzero raw eye trigram is a nonzero vector of `F5^3`. Quotienting by
nonzero scalar multiplication gives the projective plane

```text
PG(2,5): (5^3 - 1) / (5 - 1) = 31 points.
```

Canonical normalization makes the first nonzero coordinate one. The visible
labels `1..82` realize **all 31 projective points**, with representative
multiplicities

```text
17 points appear twice
 8 points appear three times
 6 points appear four times
```

The missing `83..124` states are exactly the remaining scalar representatives:

```text
17*2 + 8*1 + 6*0 = 42.
```

This is not the previous unordered-triple quotient. It identifies scalar
multiples such as `(1,2,3)` and `(2,4,1)`, whereas the multiset quotient
identifies coordinate permutations.

Two further exceptional count matches are structural rather than arithmetic
equalities:

- every line of `PG(2,5)` contains `5+1=6` points, matching the five renderer
  eyes plus newline;
- `S6` has a unique outer-automorphism class. It maps a standard
  point-stabilizing `S5` to the exotic transitive action
  `PGL(2,5)` on the six points of `P1(F5)`. The independently recovered Q
  headers generate exactly a standard `S5`.

Finally,

```text
|P1(F25)| = 25 + 1 = 26,
```

matching every complete authored row-pair record. This gives a specific
within-row Reed–Solomon test, distinct from the already-negative
**cross-panel** polynomial-share test.

These identities justify bounded hypotheses; they are not evidence that the
developers used finite geometry.

## First frozen batch

Implement four different objects, only after this horizon is committed:

1. **A — `PG(2,5)` rays:** canonical quotient inventory, exact projected
   context functionality/collinearity, and a separately calibrated
   31-symbol homophonic projection score.
2. **D — typed Dyck syntax:** exact stack validity under the frozen four
   header/pairing conventions.
3. **E — rotor router:** exact five local successor cycles learned on P and
   applied to Q.
4. **C — `P1(F25)` rows:** exact low-degree extended row-code test.

### A freeze

- Zero is a separate sentinel; it is never divided by a scalar.
- A nonzero vector is normalized by multiplying by the inverse of its first
  nonzero coordinate.
- The scalar tag is that first nonzero coordinate.
- No coordinate permutation is selected: projective incidence is invariant
  under it.
- Projected context functionality and collinearity are exact prerequisites
  before a `PGL(3,5)` fit.
- The 31-symbol language statistic is calibrated on a planted canonical ray
  homophone and reselected under every global-label control. It does not use a
  hand-assigned point-to-letter alphabet.

### D freeze

- Use the exact six-symbol renderer tape with only the spatial header eyes
  removed.
- Candidate order is header or inverse header.
- Positions `0,1,2` are opens. Positions `3,4,5` are closes, paired aligned or
  reversed: four candidates total.
- A message may end with a nonempty stack, so it need only be a **Dyck
  prefix**. Underflow or a wrong closing type is a literal contradiction.
- The complete candidate is selected by valid-prefix length on P, then held
  fixed on Q.

### E freeze

- Delete the first raw trigram only.
- For each current direction `0..4`, collect the following direction at every
  occurrence within a panel.
- One global five-successor permutation is shared for that current direction.
  Cyclic rotations are the same rotor schedule.
- Each panel may reset to a different phase, but it may not reverse or replace
  the schedule.
- P must admit all five cycles exactly before Q is scored.

### C freeze

- Split each complete 26-trigram row-pair into one ordered eye pair in `F25`
  and one unused control digit.
- Enumerate the ten monic irreducible quadratics, six ordered eye pairs,
  natural/reversed record order, and maximum degrees `0..5`.
- The first 25 columns are the lexicographically enumerated finite field
  positions; column 26 is infinity.
- One representation/degree is selected on P complete records and applied
  unchanged to Q.
- A planted extended codeword must recover its field, eye pair, direction, and
  degree before any Eye result is admissible.
