# Seventh wide funnel — dynamic transforms and self-indexing

## Purpose

This pass starts wide again after the direct factoradic-body interfaces and the
giant-dollar audit. It does not deepen the most picturesque survivor. Six
mechanisms that make materially different predictions receive one cheap test
each before any is extended.

“Project-new” below means absent from the public Discord material and local
research ledger checked so far. It cannot prove that nobody has considered an
idea privately. The tests use no plaintext crib, English score, Gate number,
or fitted letter map.

## Frozen facts and controls

- The nine headers are ordinary lexicographic `S6` ranks over
  `[center,up,right,down,left,newline]`; their finite type/coset structure is
  independently reproduced.
- Applying those permutations directly to body glyphs, products, quotients,
  cosets, running states, or newline positions was negative.
- The marker layer independently produced an exact cyclic BWT object, so BWT
  vocabulary is admissible without claiming that the bodies are BWT rows.
- The bodies have a fixed equality/prefix tree and no adjacent equal values.
  A control must preserve those facts whenever they can affect its statistic.
- Canonical numeric labels `0..82`, base-five digits, visual rows, and the
  modulus-101 embedding are developer-authored. A global permutation of body
  labels is the matched control for a proposed relation to those absolute
  labels; the headers remain frozen.
- For statistics determined only by occurrence/equality structure, use the
  established prefix-tree-preserving positional shuffle instead.
- Every lane reselects its small stated convention family inside every
  control. Promotion requires a corrected tail below `0.01` and a second
  held-out or independently authored consequence.

The six lanes are intentionally heterogeneous: alphabet transform, positional
transposition, modular side channel, coordinate derivative, equality-only
pointer tape, and ordinal self-description. A hit in one does not license
parameter expansion in the others.

## Deferred independent lanes

The remaining sixth-expansion ideas are still on the ledger and are not
replaced by this portfolio:

- synchronizing/error-detecting properties of the 83-of-125 codebook;
- without-replacement 26/83-card packets;
- reconvergence cocycles on the equality graph;
- straight-line phrase grammar beyond copied prefixes;
- only historically eligible Noita PRNG trajectories;
- model-based range coding using an independently authored frequency table.

They become a second batch only if all A–F close or if a new external selector
promotes one. This prevents a result-free first batch from silently deleting
the broader queue.

## Stop rules

- Lane A must beat controls on a collation-sensitive BWT statistic. A readable
  preview selected after the fact is not sufficient.
- Lane B must create structure beyond the known copied prefixes. One accidental
  double or a favourable six-block phase is insufficient; phase zero at the
  header/body boundary is frozen.
- Lanes C and D must show sequential structure, not merely reproduce a biased
  marginal bit count or the known Hamming histogram.
- Lane E must beat the full equality-aware positional null; a short pointer
  alphabet caused only by label frequency does not count.
- Lane F needs several exact header-record matches or a corrected rare excess;
  one of roughly one thousand eligible windows matching by chance does not
  count.

If no lane crosses `0.01`, record all six negatives and widen to the deferred
batch or return to practice-cipher method calibration. Do not rescue the best
ordinary p-value with offsets, arbitrary alphabets, or per-message choices.
